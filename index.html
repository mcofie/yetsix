Leila
  • Overview
  • Calculate missed revenue

  • Predict, plan, and prepare for patient no-shows

  • Get the insights that help you eliminate the effects created by no-shows at your institution. Leila offers the control and flexibility to help you predict and prepare for no-shows. With Leila, you'll increase productivity, not overwhelm your staff, and provide exceptional experience for patients.


  • What's new in Leila?

  • Patients can now instantly schedule appointments,order prescriptions and view test results. Doctors can share educational content (video/articles) with patients. Collaborating with and messaging fellow doctors or patients is now easy.

  • Exceptional Experience

    • Extend the boundary of service and delivery of healthcare to your patients. 21st century healthcare requires you to do more.

  • Collaboration

  • Collaborating with fellow physicians has never been easier. Conveniently share scans and documents with fellow physicians. Include your staff in conversations and plan community engagement events.

  • Deepen Relationships

  • Connect with patients during and after visits. Provide patients with relevant content that helps them achieve their health goals while reinforcing your brand with patients. Leila’s Artificial Intelligence makes it easy to create relevant video or text content for patients.

  • Convenience

  • Your patients have busy lives, and we understand. Make it easy for patients to reach you, cancel or reschedule appointments and watch your institution and commitments to keeping appointments soar.

  • Productivity

  • Equip your team to handle surges and decline in demand. Leila makes it easy to prepare and not overwhelm your team in such situations. You’ll efficiently manage your team's scarce resource — time— and create an exceptional experience for your patients on their journey to meet you.


Leila makes it easy to schedule with confidence, so you can focus on providing the care patients need.
